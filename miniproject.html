Prof. Ayomide Ojutola is the Chairman of Portland Comprehensive College

Prof.Ayomide holds a Bachelors’ Degree in Biological Sciences, a Masters’ Degree in Curriculum and Instruction,
an MBA in International Business (USA), and a Doctoral Degree in Educational Leadership.
​He was an adjunct Professor at the graduate schools at California State University, Dominguez Hills, and National University, Costa Mesa, California.
He was also a visiting scholar at Peabody College, Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee, and is the President of TOL Consult, USA.
He is the founding Partner and President of Centre for Education Reform and Sustainability Initiative (CERSI) and the California Association of Nigerian Educators (CANE).
Prof. Ayomide serves on the Governing Council of Chrisland University, Abeokuta, Nigeria.
He is the Senior Infrastructure Advisor with PDF II (a DFID Funded Program) to the Office of the Vice President and Federal Minister of Power, Works and Housing. He is also the Senior Advisor to the Executive Director, Globethics.net, Geneva, Switzerland.
